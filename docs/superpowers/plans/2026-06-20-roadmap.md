## Execution rules
- One checkpoint at a time; verify (run tests / inspect artifact) before advancing.
- Heavy compute (CP2 local inference, CP5 training) runs as long background processes — monitor, don't block.
- Append every checkpoint outcome to `docs/PROGRESS.md` (durable ledger).
- **Context guard:** at ~800k/1M, halt cleanly, ensure artifacts durable, present work-to-date.
